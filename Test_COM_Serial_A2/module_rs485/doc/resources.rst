Resource Requirements
=====================

+---------------+-------+
| Resource      | Usage |
+===============+=======+
| Memory        |  1.5K |
+---------------+-------+
| Channelends   |   2   |
+---------------+-------+
| Timers        |   1   |
+---------------+-------+
| Clockblocks   |   0   |
+---------------+-------+
| Logical Cores |   1   |
+---------------+-------+
| 1-Bit Ports   |   1   |
+---------------+-------+
| 4-Bit Ports   |   1*  |
+---------------+-------+


* 1 bit of a 4-bit port is used for direction control. This can easily be moved to a 1-bit port should a 4-bit port not be available.
